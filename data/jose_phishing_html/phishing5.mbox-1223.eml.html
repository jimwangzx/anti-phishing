Bank of America: Card Protection System

You are requested to participate in the Card Protection System to secure your Bank of America debit card. This is important to you as without your participation you will not be insured against theft or fraud with your Bank of America debit or credit card.

Please complete the online form and confirm that you had your debit or credit card at all times in your possession.

As a security measure, you are advised to complete the form within a timespan of 24-48 hours.
Bank of America may temporary block your debit or credit card if you not comply with this. This alert has been sent to you based on your preferences. If you would like to make any changes to your Online Banking Alerts service, please sign in to Online Banking and visit the Manage Alerts section.
